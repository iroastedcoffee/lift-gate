For IEEE members, please enter your membership number and select the registration fee for the correct grade (the system makes a distinction between student member and graduate student member, and also between life member, life senior member, and life fellow grade).

Non-members are encouraged to become an IEEE member. Half-year dues are in effect: New members joining between March 1 and August 15 are billed at half off the full-year membership dues, i.e., $94 for regular members and $16 for student members. After August 15, new (student) members are billed the full membership dues, valid for the remainder of 2013 as well as 2014 (i.e., up to 15 months for the price of 12 months). For more information on becoming an IEEE member, please check Join IEEE.
