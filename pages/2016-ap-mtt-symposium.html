2016 AP/MTT Symposium and Mini-Show

2016-07-21

31st Annual Symposium and Mini-Show

The AP/MTT Joint Chapter holds their 31st Annual Symposium and Mini-Show on Thursday October 6, 2016 in the Hanover Manor in Hanover, NJ. The program consists of technical sessions from 8:50 am until 4:40 pm as well as a mini-show that features the latest products (9:30 am until 4:30 pm).

The symposium program is now available.

Pre-registration is recommended to provide the organizers with an accurate headcount. Registration is on-site.

All are welcome. IEEE membership is not required. Registration is
on-site. There is no charge to attend the symposium or mini-show. A complementary
breakfast will be provided and lunch is included for all.
