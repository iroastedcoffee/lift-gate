This Chapter is a chapter of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS). The mission statement of IEEE CIS is to advance nature-inspired computational paradigms in science and engineering. The field of interest of IEEE CIS is the theory, design, application, and development of biologically and linguistically motivated computational paradigms emphasizing neural networks, connectionist systems, genetic algorithms, evolutionary programming, fuzzy systems, and hybrid intelligent systems in which these paradigms are contained.
