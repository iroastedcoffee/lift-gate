The IEEE Life Members Affinity Group, supports activities of interest to Life members. Its objectives include keeping Life members active and engaged, and in providing the Life members’ invaluable repository of personal experiences to the IEEE community.

To qualify for Life Member status, a member must be at least 65 years of age and have been a member of the IEEE or one of its predecessor societies for such a period that the sum of his/her age and his/her years of membership equals or exceeds 100 years.

Please contact the IEEE Contact Center [link] with questions regarding your IEEE Life Member status.
