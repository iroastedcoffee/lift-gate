2018 IEEE North Jersey Advanced Communications Symposium – Sep. 15, 2018

The 6th IEEE North Jersey Advanced Communications Symposium (NJACS) will be held at the Babbio Center, Stevens Institute of Technology, in Hoboken, NJ, on Saturday September 15, 2018. The symposium consists of four invited presentations and a parallel poster session.

Registration for this event is required. Please check the registration page for more information.
