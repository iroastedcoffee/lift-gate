IEEE North Jersey Technology Management Council Chapter

Apratim Rajendra, Chair
Vaseem Ahmed, Co-Chair
Tony Almeida, Past Chair

The IEEE Technology Management Council(Engineering Management) is a professional committee of IEEE. Its purpose is to bring together those who manage technology, engineering and innovation (including non-engineers); engineers who hold strategic leadership responsibilities (even if outside of the practice of engineering); and upward-bound engineers with an interest in management as a profession.
