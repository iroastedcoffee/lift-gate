AP/MTT Chapter

2012-01-29

Antennas and Propagation/Microwave Theory and Techniques Society Chapter

Ajay Poddar, Chair
Edip Niver, Vice-Chair

This Chapter is a joint chapter of the IEEE Antenna and Propagation (AP-S) Society and the Microwave Theory and Techniques (MTT) Society. The AP/MTT Chapter focuses on experimental and theoretical advances in antennas and propagation of electromagnetic waves as well as the development and application of microwave technology.
