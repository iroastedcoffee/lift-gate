2013-iSTEP

2013-10-06

iSTEP, the Integrated Student Transition to Engineering/Technology Professional program, provides students, industry professionals, and IEEE leaders the opportunity to share career experiences at one venue. Learn how IEEE can shape your future, and provide career development tools to help you succeed, at our iSTEP mini-conference listed below. Attend and glean wisdom from local industry and IEEE leaders in mentoring breakout sessions where they can help you navigate through the professional world to realize your aspirations.

The iSTEP event will be held on October 16, 2013 at Fairleigh Dickinson University in Teaneck, NJ.
