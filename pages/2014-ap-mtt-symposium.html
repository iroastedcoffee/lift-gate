2014 AP-MTT Symposium

2014-06-18

29th Annual Symposium and Mini-Show

The AP/MTT Joint Chapter holds their 29th Annual Symposium and Mini-Show on Thursday October 2, 2014 in the Hanover Manor in Hanover, NJ. The program consists of technical sessions from 8:50 am until 4:40 pm as well as a mini-show that features the latest products (9:30 am until 4:30 pm).

The symposium program is now available. Please check the symposium flyer [pdf].

Registration is on-site. Further details can also be found in the IEEE North Jersey Newsletter.

All are welcome. IEEE membership is not required. Registration is
on-site. There is no charge to attend the symposium or mini-show. A complementary
breakfast will be provided and lunch is included for all.
