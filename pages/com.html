The IEEE North Jersey Communications Society Chapter organizes local workshops and seminars. It is a part of the IEEE North Jersey Section and the IEEE Communications Society. The IEEE Communications Society fosters original work in all aspects of communications science, engineering, and technology. It encourages the development of applications that uses signals to transfer voice, data, image and/or video information between locations. It promotes the theory and use of communication systems involving all types of terminals, computers and information processors.

More information on the IEEE Communications Society can be found at http://www.comsoc.org.
