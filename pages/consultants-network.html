Consultants’ Network of Northern New Jersey

Peter Schutz, Chair
Jim Boyd, Vice-Chair
Robert Walker, Secretary, Treasurer

The Consultants’ Network of Northern New Jersey (CNNNJ) is part of the IEEE North Jersey Section. It was founded in 1992. The network is a voluntary association of independent practitioners that does not charge for its services or contract for services.

More information about the Consultants’ Network can be found at: CNNNJ website].
