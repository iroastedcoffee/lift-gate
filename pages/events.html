2011-11-02

The following events are organized by the North Jersey Section and its Chapters and Groups.

EXCOM meetings

The Executive Committee (EXCOM) of the IEEE North Jersey Society meets on the first Wednesday of each month. The meeting agenda typically includes reports from the Secretary and Treasurer, reports from the Chapter and Affinity Group Chairs and Representatives, Committee Chairs, news related to the IEEE and the North Jersey Section, planning and new initiatives. Everyone is welcome to attend the EXCOM meetings.

VTOOLS

For IEEE members, it is best to use vtools to view the events and to register for selected events by following the link [vtools-members] and using their IEEE web account to see the Section’s events. For non-members, it is best to follow the link: [vtools-non-members].
