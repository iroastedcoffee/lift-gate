IEEE Computational Intelligence Society Chapter

Eui-Hyeock Yang, Chair

This Chapter is a chapter of the IEEE Nanotechnology Council (NC). IEEE NC is a multi-disciplinary group whose purpose is to advance and coordinate work in the field of Nanotechnology carried out throughout the IEEE in scientific, literary and educational areas. The Council supports the theory, design, and development of nanotechnology and its scientific, engineering, and industrial applications.
